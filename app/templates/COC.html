LTC John Lai Guoquan

Outgoing Commanding Officer

Lieutenant Colonel (LTC) John Lai Guoquan enlisted into the Singapore Army in July 2005. A Signal Officer by vocation, he commissioned in 2006. He obtained his Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science at the Nanyang Technological University in 2010. LTC Lai was later awarded the prestigious SAF Postgraduate Scholarship in 2015. He then pursued a Master of Science in Computer Science from the US Naval Postgraduate School, as well as a Master of Science in Defence Technology and Systems from the National University of Singapore, Temasek Defence Systems Institute.

LTC Lai previously served his command tours as a Platoon Commander in 10 C4I Bn, and later as Officer Commanding, Command Systems Company in 17 C4I Bn. He also served several staff tours in the Army Command Systems Group and HQ Signals & Command Systems.

In 2020, LTC Lai completed his Command and Staff Course at the Goh Keng Swee Command and Staff College.

As CO, He led the battalion with tenacity and built a cohesive unit. He anchored the C4I Cell during Exercise DECISIVE EDGE 22 in Feb 22, and JIT-BCT 22 in Oct 22, assisting 4 SAB to develop concept and capabilities for the Multi-Domain Brigade, at the same time, being the bridge between 4 SAB with both Hq Signal and HQ Army Intelligence with regard to next generation capabilities and development.

MAJ Perry Ng Chun How

Incoming Commanding Officer

Major (MAJ) Perry Ng Chun How enlisted in the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) in 2008, and was commissioned as a Signal Officer in the same year. MAJ Ng went on to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Project and Facilities Management from the National University of Singapore, School of Design and Environment in 2013. He was later awarded the SAF Postgraduate Scholarship in 2017, and pursued a Master of Science in Instructional Systems and Learning Technologies from Florida State University, USA.

MAJ Ng served his command tours as a Platoon Commander in 8 Signal Bn, and later Officer Commanding of the Command Systems Company in 17 C4I Bn. He served various staff tours in HQ Signals & Command Systems, as well as at TRADOC.

MAJ Ng completed his Command and Staff College in 2022 at the Goh Keng Swee Command and Staff College.

12 C4I Bn is honoured to welcome MAJ Ng as the new Commanding Officer!
