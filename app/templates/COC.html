LTC John Lai Guoquan

Outgoing Commanding Officer

Lieutenant Colonel (LTC) John Lai Guoquan enlisted into the Singapore Army in July 2005. A Signal Officer by vocation, he commissioned in 2006. He obtained his Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science at the Nanyang Technological University in 2010. LTC Lai was later awarded the prestigious SAF Postgraduate Scholarship in 2015. He then pursued a Master of Science in Computer Science from the US Naval Postgraduate School, as well as a Master of Science in Defence Technology and Systems from the National University of Singapore, Temasek Defence Systems Institute.

LTC Lai previously served his command tours as a Platoon Commander in 10 C4I Bn, and later as Officer Commanding, Command Systems Company in 17 C4I Bn. He also served as S3, 2 PDF Signal Unit, and in several staff tours in HQ Signals & Command Systems.

In 2020, LTC Lai completed his Command and Staff Course at the Goh Keng Swee Command and Staff College.

As Commanding Officer, he led the battalion with tenacity and saw the completion of the 5th Mono. During his tour, he also headed the C4I Cell during the brigade-level exercises conducted in Feb 22 and Oct 22. As part of the exercises, he also assisted 4 SAB in the development of new concepts and capabilities for the Multi-Domain Brigade, by working with 4 SAB, HQ Signals & Command Systems and HQ Army Intelligence closely.

MAJ Perry Ng Chun How

Incoming Commanding Officer

Major (MAJ) Perry Ng Chun How enlisted into the Singapore Army in Jan 2008, and was commissioned as a Signal Officer in the same year. MAJ Ng went on to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Project and Facilities Management from the National University of Singapore, School of Design and Environment in 2013. He was later awarded the SAF Postgraduate Scholarship in 2017, and pursued a Master of Science in Instructional Systems and Learning Technologies from Florida State University, USA.

MAJ Ng served his command tours as a Platoon Commander in 8 Signal Bn, and later Officer Commanding of the Command Systems Company in 17 C4I Bn. He served various staff tours in HQ Signals & Command Systems, as well as at TRADOC.

MAJ Ng completed his Command and Staff College in 2022 at the Goh Keng Swee Command and Staff College.

12 C4I Bn is honoured to welcome MAJ Ng as the new Commanding Officer!

About 12 C4I Bn

12th Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance Battalion (12 C41 Bn) is the Battalion that plans, executes and sustains C4I operations as part of 4th Singapore Armoured Brigade's(4 SAB) mechanised operations.

12 C4I Bn compromises of two active mono-intake Companies, Reconnaissance, Surveillance, and Target Acquisition (RSTA) Company and Signal Company, and one active evergreen SATCOM Company, as well as one inactive Tactical Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (TUAV) Company.
